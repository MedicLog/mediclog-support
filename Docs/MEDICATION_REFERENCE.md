## About This Reference

This document lists the **37 medications** included in MedicLog's default formulary. These represent common EMS medications across various certification levels and protocols.

**Important Safety Notes:**
- Default doses shown are **examples only** - always follow your agency protocols
- Routes must match medication concentration (e.g., Epi 1:1000 is IM/SQ ONLY, never IV)
- Medication timers are organizational preferences, NOT clinical recommendations
- Verify all medications, doses, and routes with medical control when required

## Cardiac Medications

### Adenosine
- **Default Dose:** 12 mg
- **Default Route:** IV
- **Alternative Routes:** IO
- **Timer:** None
- **Use:** SVT conversion

### Amiodarone
- **Default Dose:** 300 mg
- **Default Route:** IV
- **Alternative Routes:** IO
- **Timer:** None
- **Use:** Ventricular arrhythmias, refractory VF/VT

### Atropine
- **Default Dose:** 0.5 mg
- **Default Route:** IV
- **Alternative Routes:** IO, IM
- **Timer:** 4 minutes (for repeat dosing)
- **Use:** Symptomatic bradycardia

### Epinephrine 1:10,000 (Epi 1:10,000)
- **Default Dose:** 1 mg
- **Default Route:** IV
- **Alternative Routes:** IO, ET
- **Timer:** 4 minutes (for repeat cardiac doses)
- **Use:** Cardiac arrest, severe anaphylaxis
- **‚ö†Ô∏è SAFETY:** IV/IO concentration only - never give 1:10,000 IM/SQ

### Nitroglycerin Spray (NTG Spray)
- **Default Dose:** 0.4 mg
- **Default Route:** SL (sublingual)
- **Alternative Routes:** None
- **Timer:** 4 minutes (for repeat doses)
- **Use:** Chest pain, pulmonary edema
- **Note:** Check BP before each dose

## Respiratory Medications

### Albuterol
- **Default Dose:** 2.5 mg
- **Default Route:** Nebulized
- **Alternative Routes:** IN (rare)
- **Timer:** 5 minutes (treatment duration)
- **Use:** Bronchospasm, asthma, COPD

### Atrovent (Ipratropium)
- **Default Dose:** 0.5 mg
- **Default Route:** Nebulized
- **Alternative Routes:** None
- **Timer:** None
- **Use:** COPD, bronchospasm (often combined with albuterol)

### Epinephrine 1:1000 (Epi 1:1000)
- **Default Dose:** 0.3 mg
- **Default Route:** IM
- **Alternative Routes:** SQ
- **Timer:** 10 minutes (for reassessment/repeat)
- **Use:** Anaphylaxis, severe asthma
- **‚ö†Ô∏è SAFETY:** IM/SQ concentration only - NEVER give 1:1000 IV/IO

### Narcan (Naloxone)
- **Default Dose:** 0.4 mg
- **Default Route:** IN (intranasal)
- **Alternative Routes:** IV, IM, IO
- **Timer:** None
- **Use:** Opioid overdose reversal

### Racemic Epinephrine
- **Default Dose:** 0.5 mL
- **Default Route:** Nebulized
- **Alternative Routes:** None
- **Timer:** None
- **Use:** Croup, upper airway swelling

## Medication Safety Features

### Duplicate Detection

MedicLog automatically detects potential duplicate medication entries:

- **Same medication** within 5 minutes
- **Same dose** and route
- **Different providers** (multi-medic scenarios)

**Why this matters:**
- Prevents accidental double-documentation
- Critical during provider transitions
- Reduces risk of overdose at receiving hospital

**Resolution options:**
- Confirm (intentional repeat dose)
- Keep first entry (discard duplicate)
- Keep second entry (first was error)
- Keep both (truly separate administrations)
